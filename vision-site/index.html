The Origin

Half his life on the floor. The other half building AI.

"Music is how the brothers communicate. This is not a toy or a demo. It is a working production suite built on real APIs, with real audio processing, real MIDI generation, and real AI-driven DJ intelligence."

Peter Wilson was a DJ and club promoter for half his life. He ran clubs, sold tickets, managed guest lists, and built floors. He knows what makes a crowd move, what kills a set, and why most DJs never get signed.

His brother Jimmy (VJ Wilson) DJs through headphones because his sons Liam and Logan are sleeping. Each of Jimmy's mixes tells a story of what was happening in his life when he recorded it. "Back Pain." "Mommy Night Out." "So Daddy." Sixteen mixes. Sixteen moments.

Simple Balance Music encodes all of that knowledge into software. The energy flow rules, the harmonic compatibility, the BPM transitions, the mood matching. Everything Peter learned behind the decks is now an AI that thinks like a DJ.

Named after J.A.W. Jimmy And Wilson. The brothers who communicate through music.

AI Mastering
